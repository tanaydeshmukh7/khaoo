Chicken 65

The legendary South Indian appetizer ‚Äî crispy, spicy, deep-fried chicken bites with a tangy kick, tempered with curry leaves and red chilies for an addictive flavor.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into bite-sized cubes)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 egg (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Salt to taste
  • Red food color (optional, for vibrant color)

For Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 4-5 dried red chilies
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 onion, sliced (optional)

For Frying:

  • Oil for deep frying

For Garnish:

  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Lemon wedges
  • Onion rings

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Cut boneless chicken into bite-sized cubes and wash thoroughly.
    • In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, black pepper, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt.
    • Add cornflour and all-purpose flour to the marinade.
    • If using, add beaten egg and a pinch of red food color.
    • Coat the chicken pieces well with the marinade.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (1-2 hours for best results).
  2. Prepare for Frying:
    • Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai for frying.
    • The oil should be medium-hot (not smoking).
    • Test by dropping a small piece of batter ‚Äî it should sizzle and rise immediately.
  3. First Fry:
    • Carefully add marinated chicken pieces to the hot oil in batches.
    • Don't overcrowd the pan.
    • Fry on medium heat for 5-6 minutes until chicken is cooked through and lightly golden.
    • Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. Second Fry (for Extra Crispiness):
    • Increase the oil temperature to high.
    • Add the partially fried chicken pieces back to the oil.
    • Fry for 2-3 minutes until deep golden and crispy.
    • Remove and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  5. Prepare the Tempering:
    • Heat 2 tbsp oil and butter in a separate pan.
    • Add curry leaves and let them splutter.
    • Add dried red chilies and fry for 30 seconds.
    • Add chopped garlic and green chilies, saut√© until garlic turns golden.
    • If using onions, add sliced onions and saut√© until translucent.
  6. Toss & Combine:
    • Add the fried chicken pieces to the tempering.
    • Toss everything together on high heat for 1-2 minutes.
    • The chicken should be well-coated with the aromatic tempering.
    • Turn off the heat.
  7. Serve:
    • Transfer to a serving plate.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and onion rings.
    • Serve hot with lemon wedges.
    • Perfect as an appetizer or side dish with drinks!
