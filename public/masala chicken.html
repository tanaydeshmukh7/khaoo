Ingredients

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Wash and clean the chicken pieces thoroughly.
    • Mix chicken with turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Let it marinate for 10-15 minutes while you prepare other ingredients.
  2. Prepare the Base:
    • Heat oil or ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan or pressure cooker.
    • Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, cardamoms, and cinnamon stick.
    • Let them splutter and release their aroma (30 seconds).
  3. Saut√© Onions:
    • Add finely chopped onions and saut√© on medium heat until golden brown (8-10 minutes).
    • Don't rush this step ‚Äî well-browned onions add depth to the gravy.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Tomatoes & Spices:
    • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy (5-7 minutes).
    • Add coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
    • Cook the masala until oil separates from the mixture (3-4 minutes).
  5. Cook the Chicken:
    • Add the marinated chicken pieces and mix well with the masala.
    • Cook on high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Add ¬Ω cup water (adjust for desired gravy consistency).
    • Cover and cook on medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes until chicken is tender.
    • If using a pressure cooker, cook for 2 whistles and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Final Touches:
    • Once chicken is cooked, add garam masala and black pepper powder.
    • Crush kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle over the curry.
    • Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
    • Check seasoning and adjust salt if needed.
  7. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Serve hot with roti, naan, paratha, or steamed rice.
    • Add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra zing!
