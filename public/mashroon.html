Mushroom Do Pyaza

Flavorful mushrooms cooked with double the onions in a spicy, aromatic gravy. A quick, hearty vegetarian dish that's restaurant-style and ready in under 40 minutes!

Start Cooking

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

25 mins

Serves

4 People

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

For the Main Dish:

  • 400g button mushrooms, cleaned and quartered
  • 4 large onions (2 finely chopped for base, 2 sliced into thick rings for "do pyaza")
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

For the Masala:

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ¬Ω tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ¬Ω tsp amchur (dry mango powder) or 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped for garnish

Optional Add-Ins:

  • ¬Ω cup bell peppers or capsicum, cubed (for color and crunch)
  • ¬º cup yogurt or cream (for a richer gravy)
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, crushed)

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

  1. Prep the Ingredients:
    • Clean the mushrooms by wiping with a damp cloth (avoid soaking). Trim stems if tough and quarter them.
    • Prepare onions: Finely chop 2 onions for the base. Slice the remaining 2 into thick rings or half-moons for the "do pyaza" effect.
    • Puree the tomatoes. Keep ginger-garlic paste, chilies, and spices ready.
    • If using bell peppers, cube them and set aside.
  2. Saut√© the Base Onions:
    • Heat 2 tbsp oil or ghee in a wide pan or kadai over medium heat.
    • Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, and cloves. Let them sizzle for 20-30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the finely chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until golden brown (8-10 minutes). Stir frequently to avoid burning.
    • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until the raw aroma fades.
  3. Cook the Masala:
    • Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
    • Cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes, stirring until the masala thickens and oil starts separating from the edges.
    • If using yogurt or cream, whisk it in now and cook for another 2 minutes to prevent curdling.
    • Add ¬º cup water if the masala is too dry, and mix well.
  4. Add Mushrooms and Do Pyaza Onions:
    • Add the quartered mushrooms (and bell peppers if using). Toss to coat with the masala.
    • Cook uncovered on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms release moisture and soften slightly.
    • Now add the sliced onion rings. Gently mix them in ‚Äî they should remain crisp and not fully cooked through.
    • Sprinkle garam masala, amchur (or lemon juice), and kasuri methi if using. Stir and cook for 2-3 more minutes.
    • The gravy should be semi-dry to medium consistency. If too thick, add a splash of water. Taste and adjust spices/salt.
  5. Finish and Garnish:
    • Turn off the heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Let it rest for 5 minutes ‚Äî flavors will meld beautifully.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve hot with naan, roti, paratha, or steamed rice.
    • Pair with a side of raita or salad for a complete meal.
    • Perfect for weeknight dinners or as a side in thalis!

Variations & Tips ü•ò

  • Use fresh button mushrooms for best texture; don't overcook them to keep them meaty.
  • For a drier version, skip the tomato puree and use just onions with spices.
  • Add paneer cubes for a protein boost ‚Äî toss in during the last 2 minutes.
  • Vegan-friendly: Use oil instead of ghee and skip yogurt/cream.
  • The "do pyaza" magic comes from adding onions twice ‚Äî once cooked, once fresh for crunch.
  • Spice it up: Add chopped green chilies or fresh black pepper for heat.
  • Make it creamy: Stir in cashew paste or coconut milk at the end.
  • Prep tip: Slice onions uniformly for even cooking.
  • For low-carb, serve with cauliflower rice instead of naan.
  • Store leftovers in fridge for 2-3 days; reheat gently to retain mushroom texture.
  • Double the recipe for meal prep ‚Äî freezes well for up to a month.
