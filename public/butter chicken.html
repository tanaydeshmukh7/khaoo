Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For the Gravy:

  • 4-5 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ¬Ω tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • ¬Ω cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp butter

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice in a bowl.
    • Add chicken pieces and coat well with the marinade.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (2 hours for best results).
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Heat 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until slightly charred and cooked through (8-10 minutes).
    • Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Gravy Base:
    • In the same pan, add remaining butter and oil.
    • Add bay leaf, cardamom, and cloves. Saut√© for 30 seconds.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown (5-7 minutes).
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add Tomatoes & Spices:
    • Pour in the tomato puree and cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes until oil separates.
    • Add red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, and honey/sugar.
    • Cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring continuously.
  5. Create the Creamy Sauce:
    • Lower the heat and add cooked chicken pieces to the gravy.
    • Crush kasuri methi between your palms and add to the curry.
    • Pour in fresh cream and mix gently.
    • Add garam masala and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve:
    • Garnish with a dollop of fresh cream, butter, and chopped coriander.
    • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed basmati rice.
